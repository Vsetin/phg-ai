Five ways I used AI to elevate revenue management and strategy

Pacific Hospitality Group Brittany Schilling

Hotel promo and rate loading builder

Clean load packs and aligned team communication

I created a customized GPT that turns business ideas for leisure and business travel into load ready specifications. The workflow supports demand generating strategy notes manages the rate loading steps and keeps handoffs and updates consistent across teams. The result is faster turnaround and fewer corrections when sharing promotions and rate details.

  • Estimated time saved each week about three to five hours during active promo cycles
  • Productivity difference creation of load packs is about two times faster with thirty to forty percent fewer corrections and clearer cross team visibility
  • Extra learning and growth stronger grasp of channel rules fences and testing discipline plus clearer templates for future promos
Rate code scraping tool

One click comp and parity sweeps

I built a browser based bookmark utility that scrapes rate code and price data from target pages. It clarifies offer structures checks parity and can reveal competitor promo codes for review so parity issues and misloads are spotted sooner.

  • Estimated time saved each week about ninety minutes across comp scans and parity spot checks
  • Productivity difference comp reviews increase from one to three touch points per week with consistent capture and fewer copy paste errors
  • Extra learning and growth practical exposure to page structure CSS selectors and small utility scripting
Revenue knowledge growth

Study and research coach for reports and standards

I use focused study and research modes to summarize internal SOPs policies brand standards and training resources and to interpret STR and Demand 360 reports. This delivers faster insight on market performance comp positioning and week to week shifts and it helps me communicate the why in weekly updates.

  • Estimated time saved each week about two to three hours by condensing reading and turning it into step by step actions
  • Productivity difference faster answers during new tool rollouts and report deep dives with ramp time reduced by about fifty percent
  • Extra learning and growth deeper intuition for pricing levers and clearer narratives when sharing insights with stakeholders
AI assisted marketing and communication

Hotel tag line builder and guest ready copy

I use AI to craft promo titles and rate details that are eye catching and on brand and to draft pre arrival emails and guest content. Tone and wording are tuned to audiences and channels which speeds up review and keeps messaging consistent.

  • Estimated time saved each week about one to two hours on copy drafts subject lines and refinement
  • Productivity difference faster turn of promo pages and emails with stronger consistency and quick suggestions for subject line tests
  • Extra learning and growth better persuasive writing brand alignment and reusable message blocks for future campaigns
Revenue and forecasting intelligence GPT

Navigator that aligns OneYield with ProfitSage

I am building a helper that reads two Excel files compares segments balances variances and prepares notes so final entry into ProfitSage is fast and accurate. The aim is to blend historical and forward looking data for both transient and group and to streamline analysis so scenarios are reviewed sooner.

  • Estimated time saved each week about two to four hours in forecast weeks by automating compare match and variance commentary
  • Productivity difference quicker scenario review with clear deltas by segment and fewer manual reconciles before entry
  • Extra learning and growth tighter link between demand drivers forecast choices and accounting impacts
Bonus agent mode for quick web scans

Faster comp rate and market pulse checks

I use agent style runs to collect rate examples events and competitor snapshots in one pass and then I capture the sources so follow up is simple.

  • Estimated time saved each week about one hour on rate shops and comp set sweeps
  • Productivity difference more frequent pulse checks with tidy source notes that support faster action
  • Extra learning and growth clearer view of competitor strategies and seasonal patterns across properties

Estimates reflect average weeks and will vary with promo cadence and forecast cycles.
